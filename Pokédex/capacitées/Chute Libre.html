If it does not succeed, and you maintain your concentration, you may use your action on your next turn to lift the creature into the air, dropping it to the ground for 2d10 + MOVE flying damage. Flying-type creatures or ones under the effect of Levitate, Magnet Rise or other similar abilities/moves are not damaged.
