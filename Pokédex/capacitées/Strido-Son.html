TODO TRADUIRE You emit an ear-shattering high pitched sound that only your target can hear, leaving them in a defenseless state. When activating this move, a target must make a CON saving throw against your Move DC. On a failure, all attack rolls against the target are given a +5 bonus until the end of your next turn.
