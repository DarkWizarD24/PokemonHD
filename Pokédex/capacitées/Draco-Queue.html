TODO TRADUIRE You whip your tail at an opponent in an attempt to send them away. The target must make a CON save against your Move DC. On a failure, they take 1d10 + MOVE dragon damage and are too frightened to remain in battle. In trainer battles, the target must be switched out if possible. In wild battles, the target only flees if it a lower level than the user.
